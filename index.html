Cutting-Edge Drones for Your Operation

DJI T40

The workhorse of our fleet. This multirotor sprayer boasts a 40‑litre tank, high flow rates and precision variable‑rate technology to treat row crops and orchards efficiently.

DJI T50

An evolution of the T40 platform with increased payload capacity and improved spray distribution. Ideal for large acreage row crops and higher throughput.

EAVision J100

Specialising in rugged terrain, the J100 offers forward‑looking radar and terrain following to safely deliver inputs on slopes, pastures and forested areas.

Mavic 3M

Our mapping powerhouse. The Mavic 3M captures high‑resolution RGB and multispectral imagery for orthomosaics, NDVI maps, stand counts and crop health analysis.

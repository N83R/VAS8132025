Mapping & Crop Health

Generate actionable insights with high‑resolution imagery and multispectral data. Our mapping services help you monitor crop health, optimize inputs and make informed decisions.

Service Overview

We utilize the DJI Mavic 3M multispectral drone to capture ultra‑sharp images and data across visible and near‑infrared wavelengths. Orthomosaics, NDVI and plant population analyses are delivered through an easy‑to‑interpret report, giving you a clear picture of crop performance.

Typical use cases include stand counts, crop emergence evaluation, nitrogen deficiency detection, weed pressure mapping and harvest planning.
